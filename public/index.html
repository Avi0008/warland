How to play

Rule the table without mercy.

Every player starts with $1500. Roll dice, move around the circuit, buy unowned assets, pay rent to owners, and collect $200 when you pass Launch Square.

Launch SquareCollect $200 every time you pass or land on Launch Square.
DoublesRoll doubles for a bonus roll. Three doubles sends you to Jail.
Community FundDraws a community card: grants, repairs, fees, gifts, or a Jail pass.
Market NewsDraws a market card: move spaces, collect money, pay fees, or go to Jail.
TaxesCity Tax charges $200. Luxury Tax charges $100.
Vacation CashTax payments collect on Vacation. A player who lands directly on Vacation takes the full pot.
RentOwned cities charge about 10-15% of their purchase price as base rent. Airport rent is $25 for one, $50 for two, $100 for three, and $200 for four.
JailPay $50 to leave immediately, or try for doubles for up to three turns.
AuctionsDeclined purchases immediately go to a live room auction.
SetsOwn a full color set to double base rent and unlock buildings.
BuildingsAfter owning a full color set, build houses evenly across that set. Four houses can upgrade to a hotel, and selling must stay even too.
UtilitiesElectric Supply and Water Supply cost $150 each. Owning one charges 4x the dice roll; owning both charges 10x the dice roll.
LiquidityMortgage assets for cash, then unmortgage later with interest.
DealsTrade cash and properties with any rival.
